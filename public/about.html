Our Story & Mission

How we began and what drives us forward

It all started with a simple idea: to create a platform that brings people together by making it easy to find and share and partake in local events. Because we believe that community connection is essential for a vibrant and thriving society.

So we made a simple website that helps people discover and participate in local events effortlessly. By displaying events in an organized map and showing event details clearly, also allowing others to know how many people are going to each event.

And this is only the start. We are committed to growing this network of local connection, with plans to bring our platform to communities throughout Sweden. Our goal is to become the heartbeat of local happenings nationwide, empowering even more people to turn a simple idea into a memorable experience.

🔍 Event Aggregation

We continuously scan and collect events from multiple official sources, community boards, and local organizers to bring you the most comprehensive calendar of activities in your area.

Our integrated mapping system helps you discover events near you and plan your schedule with easy-to-use visual tools that show event locations and details at a glance.

📱 Accessible Platform

We've built our platform to be fully responsive and accessible across all devices, ensuring you can discover and plan your activities whether you're at home or on the go.

We collaborate with local organizations and community leaders to ensure our platform reflects the diverse interests and needs of the people we serve.

🎯 Personalized Discovery

Our filtering and categorization system helps you find exactly what you're looking for - whether it's family activities, cultural events, sports, or educational workshops.

🆓 Free Access

We believe community connection should be accessible to everyone, which is why our platform remains completely free for both event organizers and attendees.
